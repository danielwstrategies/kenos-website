# Keno's Restaurant Website - Design Summary

**Figma File:** https://www.figma.com/design/UkjdWboPybUinpbV4H9HlK/Keno-s-Restaurant-Website?node-id=1-2&m=dev

---

## Typography

### Primary Fonts
- **Yeseva One** (Regular) - Used for headlines, navigation, buttons, and prominent text
- **Montserrat** (Medium, Bold) - Used for body text, descriptions, and secondary content

### Text Sizes
- Hero headline: 80px
- Section headlines: 36px
- Navigation/Body text: 12-20px
- Small text/captions: 10-14px

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Cream/Beige Background**: `#FFFCF8` (main page background), `#F0E3D9` (alternate section)
- **Deep Red/Burgundy**: `#980914`, `#711B1C`, `#A40D0F`, `#DB0A1A` (brand colors, accents, CTAs)
- **Dark Brown**: `#2F0C0C`, `rgba(47,12,12,0.81)` (overlay backgrounds)

### Accent Colors
- Light beige: `#F5EBEB` (secondary buttons)
- White: Used for text overlays and contrast
- Black: Text on light backgrounds

## Component Patterns

### Buttons
**Primary Button:**
- Red/burgundy background with white text
- Rounded corners (4px)
- Inner shadow effects: `inset 0px 1px 1px 0px rgba(255,255,255,0.25), inset 0px -1px 1px 0px rgba(47,0,0,0.59)`
- Padding: 24px horizontal, 10px vertical

**Secondary Button:**
- Light beige background (`#F5EBEB`) with black text
- Same rounded corners and shadow effects
- Same padding as primary

### Cards/Sections
- Rounded corners (8px, 15px, 20px depending on element)
- Drop shadows for depth: `0px 16px 34px 0px rgba(0,0,0,0.5)` for image gallery
- Image overlays with gradients

### Navigation
- Horizontal menu with Yeseva One font
- Small text (12px) in white
- Items: Current Menus & Ordering, Entertainment, History, Our Staff, Gallery, Contact, Jobs, News, Become a VIP, Sign In

---

## Layout Structure

- Fixed width: 1058-1084px content area
- Centered alignment
- Generous padding/spacing (50-100px sections)
- Absolute positioning for overlapping elements
- Responsive image galleries with consistent sizing

---

## Photography Style

- High-quality food photography throughout
- Warm, appetizing tones
- Mix of plated dishes and action shots
- Dark overlays for text readability
- Images include: fried chicken, burgers, salads, breakfast items, and restaurant interiors

---

## Key Design Principles

This design creates a warm, family-friendly restaurant brand with a classic American diner feel, using rich reds and warm creams to evoke comfort and appetite appeal. The design balances modern web aesthetics with traditional restaurant charm.

### Visual Hierarchy
1. Hero section with strong headline and food imagery
2. Awards/social proof immediately visible
3. Promotional content (Thanksgiving menus)
4. Call-to-action for online ordering
5. Food gallery showcasing variety
6. Brand story and team introduction
7. Footer with practical information

### Brand Personality
- Welcoming and community-focused
- Family-oriented
- Established and trustworthy (31+ years in business)
- Quality-driven with emphasis on food presentation

---

## Technical Notes

- Design uses fixed positioning for some elements
- Background images use gradient overlays for text readability
- Color gradients: `linear-gradient(to-r, from #711B1C to #A40D0F)`
- Box shadows and inner shadows create depth
- Border radius creates modern, friendly feel
